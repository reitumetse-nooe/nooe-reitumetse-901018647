Discover Greece

History & Culture

Greece has one of the oldest and most fascinating histories in the world. Ancient Greece was the birthplace of democracy, theater, and the Olympic Games.

Greek mythology is filled with gods, heroes, and legends that continue to inspire literature and movies today. Temples and ruins across the country are reminders of its glorious past.

Greece is located in southeastern Europe, famous for its islands, clear blue seas, and friendly people. It is known as the birthplace of democracy, philosophy, theater, and the Olympic Games.

The capital city, Athens, is home to the Acropolis and the Parthenon. Other popular places include Santorini, Mykonos, and Crete with its mountains and villages.

Greek food is loved around the world. Dishes like souvlaki, moussaka, Greek salad, and baklava are both delicious and healthy. Music, dance, and celebrations show the warm spirit of the Greek people.

Today, Greece is a travel destination where ancient history and modern life meet. Its natural beauty and culture make it one of the most unique countries in the world.

Did you know? The word "alphabet" comes from the first two Greek letters: alpha and beta.

Greece has more than 6,000 islands, but only about 200 are inhabited!
